About Us

 

With the steady development and transformation of China’s economy and capital markets, the society’s demands for professional and holistic asset management services are rising rapidly. To enable more and more successful individuals and institutions to optimize assets portfolios and monetize global investment opportunities, Lead Future Capital Co., Ltd. (“Lead Future Capital”) is founded.

Lead Future Capital is headquartered in Beijing and granted as qualified private investment fund manager by Asset Management Association of China. Our business is focused on public and private investments, investment consulting and financial advisory services.

The global markets are presenting dynamic and exciting opportunities but time will wait for no one. Lead Future Capital is dedicated to empowering you to capture the fleeting global investment opportunities and optimize your assets portfolios through our unique insights and professional investment strategies.
